About

Money Empire is an incremental clicker game built with Java. Its ported into HTML, CSS, and JS.

How To Play

Build your empire through investing, businesses, assets, and collectibles. Grow your net worth and dominate the economy.

📈 Investing

Invest in Stocks, Real Estate, and Cryptocurrency to generate passive income and grow your wealth.

  • Stocks: Buy shares, earn dividends per hour, and buy out companies.
  • Real Estate: Own properties worldwide and collect rental income.
  • Cryptocurrency: High risk, high reward assets based on market value.

🏢 Businesses

Start and manage businesses to earn consistent hourly income.

  • Maximum 20 business slots.
  • Each business increases passive earnings.
  • Higher-tier businesses generate more profit.

💳 Earnings

Earn money actively through the click system.

  • Your balance is displayed on a virtual credit card.
  • Click to earn instant money.
  • Upgrade your click value to increase earnings per tap.

🚗 Items & Assets

Collect luxury transport, residence upgrades, collectibles, and islands.

  • Garage, Hangar, Harbor for vehicles.
  • Residence stores gold, silver, and diamonds.
  • Collections increase prestige and net worth.
  • Buy private islands as elite assets.

👤 Profile & Statistics

Track your complete financial empire.

  • Total Net Worth overview.
  • Asset breakdown by category.
  • Progress tracking and ownership stats.
  • Detailed earnings breakdown.
